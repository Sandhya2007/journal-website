Publication Ethics

Thank you for considering submitting your work to the MAIT Journal of Science and Technology. Before submitting your manuscript, please ensure that you have read and understood the following guidelines:

  • Authors should ensure that their work is original and has not been published elsewhere. Any sources that have been used in the research should be properly cited.
  • All authors who have made a significant contribution to the research should be listed as authors. It is unethical to include individuals who have not contributed to the research as authors.
  • Authors should disclose any financial or personal relationships that could be perceived as potential conflicts of interest in relation to the work being submitted.
  • The editor-in-chief is responsible for deciding which articles to publish based on the recommendations of the editorial board and reviewers, and in accordance with legal requirements.
  • All submitted manuscripts will undergo a double-blind peer review process. Reviewers should disclose any conflicts of interest and should treat manuscripts confidentially.
  • Authors should follow relevant reporting standards for their research field. Any deviations from these standards should be clearly justified. Authors should ensure that their research complies with relevant ethical standards regarding human and animal rights.
  • Authors should promptly notify the journal editor if they discover an error in their published work and cooperate with the editor to retract or correct the paper. Authors should disclose all sources of funding for their research and any potential conflicts of interest that may arise from the funding source.
