Author's Guidelines

Thank you for considering submitting your work to the MAIT Journal of Science and Technology. Before submitting your manuscript, please ensure that you have read and understood the following guidelines:

Manuscript Preparation

  • Manuscripts should be prepared in English and submitted as a Microsoft Word document (.doc or .docx)..
  • Use Times New Roman font, size 12, and double-spaced throughout the manuscript.
  • Include a title page with the title of the manuscript, author(s) name(s), affiliation(s), and contact information.
  • Ensure that the manuscript follows the journal's formatting and citation style guidelines.

Manuscript Structure

All submitted manuscripts will undergo a double-blind peer review process.

Submission Process

Authors should ensure that their work is original and has not been published elsewhere.

Review Process

Submit your manuscript online through our submission system. You will be required to create an account to submit your manuscript.
